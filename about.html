About us

A practical local service for homeowners in Eau Claire.

Eau Claire Gutter Cleaning is built around a simple idea: homeowners should be able to get their gutters cleaned without overpaying or waiting through a complicated sales process.

Our approach

Keep it clear, careful, and reasonably priced.

We serve Eau Claire and nearby communities with basic gutter cleanouts, downspout cleaning, debris haul-away, and tidy cleanup. The goal is not to make the service bigger than it needs to be. It is to show up, do the work, and make the next rainstorm less stressful.

If a home has tricky access, loose gutters, or a repair issue, we will tell you what we see. If the job is a simple cleanout, we keep it simple.
